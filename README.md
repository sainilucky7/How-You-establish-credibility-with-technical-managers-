# How-You-establish-credibility-with-technical-managers:

As a technical recruiter, influencing senior leaders really comes down to not acting like someone who just takes orders. Instead, you have to be a trusted advisor who brings real information, market insight, and solutions. Leaders listen when you show them what’s happening in the talent market and how it affects their hiring goals.
1.	Influencing Senior Leaders (Using Real Data, Not Opinions) - use real market data — salary ranges, how many people actually have the skills we want, what competitors are offering
2.	Changing Hiring Strategy (Being Proactive, Not Reactive)- Sometimes the original hiring plan doesn’t work, especially for niche roles. Instead of waiting for applicants, I help leaders shift to a more proactive approach
3.	Fixing Issues in the Hiring Process (Clear Communication + Faster Decisions)- When hiring stalls, I look at the whole process to find the real problem — not just the symptoms. Slow feedback, fast offers from competitors
